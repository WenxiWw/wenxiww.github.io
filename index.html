Wenxi Wu

I am a PhD student at the Centre for Doctoral Training - Safe & Trusted AI (joint PhD programme at King's College and Imperial College London), at RRAI Lab. I had my Master's degree from the Technical University of Munich (TU München). I was working as a research intern at the Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence，advised by Dr. Luis Figueredo.

My research interests lie in the intersection of human-centered robotics and AI. My research bridges robot motion planning and explainable AI. My work focuses on helping users understand the capabilities and limits of robots using explainable methods. My ambition is to build intelligent and adaptive robot systems that assist humans in a safe and trusted way.

Research Interests: Explainable AI, Motion Planning, Human-robot Interaction, AI Fairness, Foundation Models in Robotics
